Our Story

Founded in 2010, La Casa Verde brings authentic Mexican cuisine to the heart of Minnesota. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, ensuring every dish captures the true essence of Mexican culinary tradition.

We use only the freshest ingredients, sourced locally whenever possible, and prepare everything fresh daily. From our handmade tortillas to our signature salsas, every element is crafted with care and passion.
